Biography

Julieta Venegas was born November 24,1970 in Long Beach,California.

She is a Mexican Pop-Rock singer and songwriter. Plays several instruments and is fluent in Spanish, Portugese and English.

Has won various awards, five Latin Grammys and one Grammy award. And has a twin sister, Yvonne.
